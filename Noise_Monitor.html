This bar shows the energy difference between mic and output. When it is green and above threshold, the Rust VAD gate is open and your voice passes through. When red, the gate is shut and silence is sent to the headphones.

What to look for: if the mic waveform has signal but the output waveform is flat — the gate is cutting your speech. If the output spectrum has high-frequency energy when you are not speaking — noise is leaking through.
